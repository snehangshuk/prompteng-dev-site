### üí° How Assessments Work

1. **Complete the module** in the course repository first
2. **Return here** and select the module assessment
3. **Check the skills** you've mastered
4. **Submit your progress** - your data is saved automatically
5. **View your dashboard** to see overall progress

Your progress is tracked via email, so use the same email across all assessments!
